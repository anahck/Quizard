About This App

Quizard is an interactive web application designed to help Key Stage 3 students test their knowledge in a fun and engaging way.

The app includes:

  • 🎯 Multiple-choice quizzes
  • 📊 Score tracking and feedback
  • 👤 A simple login system so each student can have their own progress saved

Why We Built This

Our goal is to make learning interactive and enjoyable for students. By combining technology with education, we encourage students to practice regularly and improve their understanding in key subjects.
